Excel with a Mentor

Partnering Military with the Civilian Sector

Mentorship is the best path to career success, hands down. The benefits that you can gain from a good mentor relationship can outweigh grad school, natural ability, and even dumb luck. The key is to have the foresight and humility to ask to be mentored. If you start here, you’ll find that there are plenty of accelerators in your life who can add value. More importantly, you can take it upon yourself to add tremendous value for them. In doing so, you’ll get the most out of the mentor relationship and find success in your life and career, sooner.

The Excel with a Mentor (EWAM) program is designed to assist in the transition of military members into the civilian workforce with the guidance and expertise of an industry professional.

Thank you for everything that you have done to serve your country and community! We are excited about your interest in this mentorship opportunity. This program will connect you with local seasoned professionals committed to working with you.

Our program will assist you with:

Career goals and objectives

Job exploration

Building networks

Career preparation

Begin your new journey today! Sign up and tell us a little about yourself and one of our members will be in contact with you, shortly.
